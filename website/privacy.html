Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2025 Version: 1.0

At S2S, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our real-time speech translation application for macOS.

Our Privacy Commitment

Your conversations are yours. S2S processes audio locally on your device whenever possible. We don't store, record, or analyze your translated content for any purpose beyond providing the translation service.

Information We Collect

Audio Data

  • Audio input from your microphone is processed in real-time
  • Audio is transmitted to ByteDance's translation API only during active translation
  • We do not store or retain audio recordings
  • All audio processing is ephemeral and deleted immediately after translation

API Credentials

  • Your ByteDance API keys are stored securely in macOS Keychain
  • Credentials are never transmitted to our servers
  • Keys are only used to authenticate with the translation service

Usage Analytics (Optional)

  • Basic usage statistics (app launches, feature usage)
  • Error logs for troubleshooting (no personal data included)
  • Performance metrics to improve service quality
  • You can opt-out of analytics at any time in preferences

How We Use Information

The limited information we collect is used exclusively to:

  • Provide real-time speech translation services
  • Improve app performance and fix bugs
  • Respond to support requests
  • Ensure service reliability and security

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption: All network communications use TLS/SSL encryption
  • Local Storage: Sensitive data stored in macOS Keychain
  • No Cloud Storage: We don't store your conversations or translations
  • Minimal Data: We only collect what's necessary for the service

Third-Party Services

ByteDance Translation API

S2S uses ByteDance's AST (Automatic Speech Translation) API to provide translation services. When you use S2S:

  • Audio is transmitted to ByteDance servers only during active translation
  • ByteDance processes audio according to their privacy policy
  • We recommend reviewing ByteDance's Privacy Policy

Your Rights and Choices

You have control over your data:

  • Access: View what data the app stores locally
  • Delete: Remove all app data by uninstalling S2S
  • Opt-out: Disable analytics in app preferences
  • API Control: Manage your own API credentials

Data Retention

S2S follows a minimal data retention policy:

  • Audio: Not retained after translation
  • Translations: Displayed only, not stored
  • Logs: Automatically deleted after 7 days
  • Preferences: Retained until app uninstallation

Children's Privacy

S2S is not intended for use by children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this policy. Continued use of S2S after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Open Source Commitment

S2S believes in transparency. Our codebase is open for review, allowing you to verify our privacy practices. You can examine exactly how we handle your data.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@s2s.app
  • GitHub: Report an Issue

California Privacy Rights

California residents have additional rights under the CCPA. S2S does not sell personal information and provides all users with the rights described in this policy regardless of location.

International Users

S2S is designed for global use. By using S2S, you consent to the processing of information as described in this policy, regardless of your country of residence.
